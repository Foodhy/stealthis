Velocity-Driven Animation

The marquee text above and below responds to your scroll speed in real time. Scroll faster and the text accelerates. Stop scrolling and it gently returns to its base speed. The direction can even reverse when you scroll up.

Smooth Interpolation

Linear interpolation (lerp) ensures the speed transitions are buttery smooth, never jerky. The velocity decays naturally when scrolling stops, creating an organic, physics-inspired feel.
