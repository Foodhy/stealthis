## NFT / Digital Art Marketplace

A dark, premium digital art marketplace with all the visual energy of a Web3 drop — gradient glows, live auction countdown, bid price tickers, and an immersive hero.

### Sections

1. **Nav** — Logo, search bar, wallet connect button
2. **Hero** — Featured drop card with animated live bid, countdown, "Place Bid" CTA
3. **Live Auctions** — Grid of NFT cards with price, time left, glow-on-hover
4. **Top Artists** — Avatar + name row, volume stats
5. **How it works** — 3-step process with numbered icons
6. **Trending** — Price chart sparklines (CSS-drawn), collection stats
7. **Footer** — Newsletter + social links

### Key interactions

- **Bid ticker** — setInterval increments bid price randomly for live feel
- **Card glow** — `box-shadow` + gradient border animates on hover
- **Auction countdown** — live DD:HH:MM:SS timer per card
- GSAP stagger grid reveal on scroll
