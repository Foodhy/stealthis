## Product Showcase

A product showcase video with an animated product name, tagline, and four feature cards that spring in with stagger. The title scales in from 0.8, the tagline slides up with a spring delay, and each feature card enters from below with increasing delay. The entire composition fades out over the last 40 frames.

### Composition specs

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Resolution | 1280 x 720 |
| FPS | 30 |
| Duration | 7 s (210 frames) |

### Customization

- **PRODUCT_NAME** — the product name displayed at the top
- **TAGLINE** — subtitle text below the product name
- **FEATURES** — array of `{ icon, title, desc }` objects for the feature cards
