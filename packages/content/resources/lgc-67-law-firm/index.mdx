## Legal / Law Firm

An authoritative, trust-first law firm website. Serif headings, restrained color palette (navy + gold), crisp layout, and copy that communicates confidence without flash.

### Sections

1. **Nav** — Firm name + est. year, practice areas dropdown, phone number CTA
2. **Hero** — Split layout: headline + "Free Consultation" form left, full-height image right
3. **Trust bar** — "50 Years of Practice · 2,000+ Cases Won · Martindale Rated AV"
4. **Practice Areas** — Clean card grid: Corporate, Litigation, Real Estate, Employment, IP
5. **Attorneys** — Profile cards with headshot placeholder, name, speciality, bar admissions
6. **Results** — Case outcome highlights with amount won
7. **Testimonials** — Text-only quotes, large serif
8. **Contact** — Full-width form + office address map placeholder

### Design language

- Fonts: `Playfair Display` (headings) + `Inter` (body)
- Colors: `#0a1628` navy, `#b8963e` gold, `#f8f7f4` warm white
- No animations — professional restraint is the point
