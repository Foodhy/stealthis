## Confetti Explosion

A satisfying **canvas-based confetti explosion** that fires colorful particles on button click. Particles exhibit realistic gravity, spin, air resistance, and gentle fade-out.

### How it works

1. Clicking the button spawns a burst of particles at the button's center
2. Each particle gets a random velocity, color, size, rotation speed, and shape
3. A `requestAnimationFrame` loop applies gravity, drag, and rotation every frame
4. Particles fade out over time and are removed once fully transparent

### Customization

- Adjust `PARTICLE_COUNT`, `GRAVITY`, `DRAG`, and `COLORS` in the script
- Change particle shapes (rectangles, circles, or confetti strips)
- Modify spread angle and initial velocity for different burst patterns

### When to use it

- Success / completion celebrations
- Game win screens
- Form submission confirmations
- Any moment that deserves a little joy
