English Text with Arabic Words

Arabic words embedded in English text flow correctly when marked with proper directionality.

The Arabic word مرحبا (marhaba) means "hello" and is commonly used as a greeting across the Arab world, from Morocco to Iraq.

In calligraphy, the بسملة (basmala) is one of the most frequently rendered phrases. It translates to "In the name of God, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful."
