Brand Redesign: From Confusion to Clarity

A complete redesign of a SaaS dashboard serving 50,000 users — reducing onboarding drop-off by 42%.

Timeline12 weeks

Team4 people
ToolsFigma, Framer

01 — Challenge

The Problem

The original dashboard had grown organically over 5 years without a coherent design vision. Users described it as "overwhelming", "confusing", and "hard to find anything in." New users took an average of 18 minutes to complete their first key action.

The goal was clear: redesign the core experience to reduce time-to-value and improve retention in the critical first 30 days.

02 — Solution

The Approach

We started with 3 weeks of user research — 24 interviews, 200 survey responses, and 40 session recordings. The core insight: users weren't failing because the features didn't exist; they couldn't find them.

The redesign focused on three pillars:

  • Progressive disclosure — show only what's needed at each stage
  • Contextual guidance — inline hints, not modal tutorials
  • Consistent patterns — one way to do each type of action
